AuLeon designs and deploys governed agent systems for RFQs, quoting, supplier coordination, lead intelligence, and other revenue-critical workflows where speed, accuracy, and auditability matter.

The Problem

Critical workflows still arrive messy.

RFQs, RFPs, supplier notes, pricing inputs, approvals, and customer requirements still move through email, PDFs, spreadsheets, and tribal knowledge. The work is too important to leave scattered, but too variable for simple automation.

Step 01 - Intake

Every document enters one controlled workflow.

The system ingests RFQs, drawings, specs, emails, spreadsheets, and supporting documents, then organizes the source material without losing context. Typed text, annotations, attachments, and routing details stay connected to the original request.

Step 02 - Structure

Messy inputs become a quote-ready record.

Requirements, line items, deadlines, assumptions, missing information, supplier needs, and review owners are extracted into a structured workspace so estimators can work from one reliable source of truth.

Step 03 - Coordinate

Supplier follow-up stops living in inboxes.

AuLeon drafts clarification requests, routes them for approval, tracks supplier responses, and maps each reply back to the relevant RFQ item. Your team keeps control while the system handles the follow-through.

Step 04 - Review & Score

Costs, risks, and exceptions surface early.

The system compares supplier inputs, lead times, certifications, pricing assumptions, and open risks. Estimators see what changed, what is missing, and where human judgment is required before a quote moves forward.

Step 05 - Assemble

A complete package, ready for human approval.

Quote forms, worksheets, clarification responses, supporting documents, and audit history are assembled into a review-ready package. Final submission stays governed, traceable, and approved by the people responsible for the outcome.

The Operating Model

Diagnose. Design. Deploy. Operate.

AuLeon does not sell open-ended AI exploration. We start with one measurable workflow, define the controls, integrate with existing systems, and operate the agent layer with human review, audit trails, and performance monitoring.

01 - Diagnose
Map the workflow
We map the workflow, source data, systems, approval points, risks, and business KPI. The goal is to find the highest-value workflow slice that can move from assessment to pilot without becoming a transformation project.
02 - Design
Define the controls
We design the agent system around your rules: what it can decide, what it must escalate, what evidence it must cite, and where humans approve the output.
03 - Deploy & Operate
Launch with oversight
We connect the workflow to your documents, tools, and systems of record, then launch with monitoring, evals, audit logs, and a clear path from pilot to production.
